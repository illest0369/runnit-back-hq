# SKILL: CAPTION FORMATTER

## PURPOSE
Format captions and hashtags for clean, high-performance posting across platforms.

---

## INPUT
- hook
- clip context

---

## OUTPUT
Return:
- caption
- hashtags

---

## CAPTION RULES

- short and readable
- broken into 1–2 lines max
- no clutter
- no over-explaining
- must match hook tone

---

## STYLE

- conversational
- confident
- minimal

---

## STRUCTURE

Line 1: hook  
Line 2 (optional): light reinforcement or context  

---

## HASHTAG RULES

- 4–6 hashtags max
- mix of:
  - broad (#nfl, #nba)
  - niche (#lakersnation, #ufcnews)
- no spam tags
- no irrelevant tags

---

## REJECTION RULES

Reject if:

- caption is too long
- too many hashtags
- feels generic or forced
- doesn’t match clip tone

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Caption:
[formatted text]

Hashtags:
[tag1 tag2 tag3 tag4 tag5]
