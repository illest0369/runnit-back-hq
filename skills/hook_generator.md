# SKILL: HOOK GENERATOR

## PURPOSE
Create high-performing hooks that stop scroll and force curiosity.

---

## INPUT
- clip context
- segment description

---

## OUTPUT
Return 3 hook options

---

## CORE RULES

- max 10 words
- must create curiosity, tension, or surprise
- no generic phrases
- no filler words
- written for immediate impact

---

## HOOK TYPES

Use one of these angles:

1. SHOCK
- unexpected moment
- “no way that just happened”

2. CONFLICT
- disagreement or challenge
- tension between people

3. CONSEQUENCE
- something went wrong or right
- stakes are clear

4. CURIOSITY GAP
- incomplete information
- forces viewer to watch

---

## REJECTION RULES

Reject hooks that:

- sound generic (“crazy moment”, “what a play”)
- explain too much
- feel slow or boring
- don’t create tension

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Hook 1: [text]
Hook 2: [text]
Hook 3: [text]
